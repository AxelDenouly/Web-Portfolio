Why have I chosen the Computer Science ?

This part of my life represents one of my greatest sources of regrets, as I am aware of my intellectual abilities. However, a lack of information and ill-advised decisions have led me to this situation.

After obtaining my high school diploma, my parents questioned me about my future plans. I responded with conviction, saying "computer science," discussing what I had always wanted to do. At the time, I was in Africa, and the idea of coming to Europe was not yet on the table. Following the advice of some people around me, I decided to steer my studies towards accounting, hoping to access a job and a better salary more quickly than in the field of computer science. However, this decision resulted in a wasted year without passing any exams.

Subsequently, the idea of coming to Europe emerged. I had to take language courses and pass a test to be eligible for a visa application. This process took almost an additional two years. Once in Germany, I continued with language courses, but a lack of information prevented me from realizing that I needed to reach a certain language level to enroll in the computer science field. Circumstances kept getting more complicated, and due to language and administrative constraints, I had to opt for a program that matched my language proficiency. Although this decision took me away from my initial ambition, I did my best to adapt.

Fortunately, destiny recently smiled upon me when I reconnected with an acquaintance who had transitioned into the field of computer science in France. This encounter enlightened me about the path I am currently on. My main obstacle was my lack of knowledge; otherwise, I would have taken action much earlier.

I'm not seeking excuses, as my choices were the result of personal decisions. However, now that I am better informed and understand the way forward, I am committed to persevering until I seize an opportunity. Starting late doesn't matter to me, as I am determined to make up for lost time through my perseverance and ability to progress rapidly.
